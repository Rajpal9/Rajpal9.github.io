I am a direct PhD scholar and Prime Minister Research Fellow at Robert Bosch Centre for Cyber-Physical Systems in Indian Institute of Science (IISc), Bengaluru.
I completed my B.Tech. in Mechanical Engineering from Delhi Technological University (DTU), Delhi in 2017.
I worked as Mechanical Design Engineer in Larsen & Toubro (L&T) Defence, Bengaluru for four years before joining IISc in 2021.
My current research areas include robust control, adaptive non-linear control, and their applications to unmanned vehicles with primary focus on developing advanced autonomous algorithms for unmanned aerial vehicles.
