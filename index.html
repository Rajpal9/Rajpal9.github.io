I am a PhD scholar and Prime Minister Research Fellow at Robert Bosch Centre for Cyber Physical Systems (RBCCPS) in IISc Bangalore.
I received by B.Tech. degree in Mechanical Engineering (2020) from National Institute of Technology, Raipur.

I started my career in the field of robotics especially on mobile robots.
My primary focus as a PhD student is on trajectory planning and optimization of mobile robots. I also work on control system, human-robot interaction and reinforcement learning for all kinds of robotic platform.
